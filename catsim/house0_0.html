The first level you start in get used to the basic controls and how to play.

When you knock over everything in the kitchen corner the woman will say "eouch!" and then start wandering then going to the window and looking at it.

If you knock over the tv in the living room where a man is sitting he will knock over the table in return then starting wandering then going to the window and looking at it.

In the bathroom, If you interact with the bathtub, You can fill it up and take a bath!

In the bedroom, You can find a closest, If you interact with the closest, You will open it, revealing coins that you can collect and some 3 pillows. (This interaction will not give you rewards or score.)


Multiplayer

When you go to the kitchen corner when you're on multiplayer, You will get 500 points and a voice will say "eouch!"


Trivia

The tutorial will only appear one-time.

The curtains in the living room were supposed to be White instead of Purple in the beta.

Cat Simulator used to give you no points for knocking over a TV in the beta.
