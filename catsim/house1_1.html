This is your first "Second Floor" level.

Touch the valve in the bathroom to create smoke that after 5 seconds it scares off a man inside it.

Interact with a vase in the bedroom to drop a vase onto the dog to make it wake up, If you go away from the bed it will start chasing you, If it touches you, It knocks you back.

Interact with the wardrobe in the bedroom to open it.

Books in the table only will count as a combo if it hits the floor.
