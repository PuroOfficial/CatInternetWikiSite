First Sign of a Reward

Has Water?:

false

NPCS:

6

Interactions:

3

Has Rewards?:

true

It's time to get a reward!

You will notice that a dog is running around, If you come closer and touch him, He will ask you to bring a steak! If you bring him something else, He will resume running and dislike that, If you bring the right item, He will eat it then go to sleep, In return you get points!

3 NPCS are sitting in a bench, If you break their dinner they will go away, to the house.

If you interact with the lawnmover, You will make it turn on by scratching it and it starts going around as the man chases it down to stop it. (Spoliers: he can't.)

Interact with the ladder to knock it down to the other side, As a man in a tree gets stuck and calls for "Help!"


Multiplayer

Lawnmover plays the same sound even if you touch it gently.


Trivia

The reward will disappear forever once you collect it but it will stay in your collection.
